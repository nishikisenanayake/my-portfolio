What I do

Where creativity meets purpose.

Design has always been my creative outlet. From crafting UI mockups to building visual content, I love the process of turning an idea into something people can see and feel. Good design, to me, isn't just about aesthetics — it's about solving problems beautifully.

My journey

How it all unfolded.

1

Discovering the passion

It started with curiosity — experimenting with Canva and Figma, creating social media visuals and slowly falling in love with the craft of design.

2

Building the skills

I started learning UI/UX principles, color theory, and typography — applying them to real projects and refining my eye for detail along the way.

3

Applying it professionally

Design became a natural extension of my BA work — creating wireframes, mockups and visual documentation that made complex ideas easier to understand.
